There will be an 11% buy and sell tax used for staking rewards and supporting product development.

We believe in giving back to the community. Therefore, 1% of all fees generated will automatically be stored in a charity wallet. At the end of each month, we will donate to a chosen charity. We will vote in our telegram community group for users to choose a charity and provide proof of transfer for total clarity on fund usage.
